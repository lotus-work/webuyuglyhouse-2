How we determine your offer

To create our valuation of your property, we consider local market conditions and neighborhood comparative sales, along with what we’ve learned about your house. We’ll do a quick check around the exterior of the house, then the bedrooms, bathrooms, water heater, HVAC system, and any other major areas at the property. Many elements go into making an offer, and you can trust that we’ve considered each carefully.

After our walkthrough, we’ll talk about how the process works and how it may fit your situation. You don’t have to do anything if we come to your house and make an offer, and you won’t be charged a cent for our visit. Making an appointment and getting a cash offer from us is free, and you’re free to consider, accept, or decline—whatever is right for you.

What to expect on our visit

Our no-obligation consultation will typically take about an hour, in which one of our property specialists will assess your home and answer any questions you may have. You don’t need to do anything before we come over to see your house—no repairs, updates, or even cleaning.
