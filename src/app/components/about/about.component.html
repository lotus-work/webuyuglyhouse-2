How we do it differently

At I Buy Ugly Houses®, we buy houses fast for cash. We work in person with sellers, so you have a familiar face to help you fully understand our process.

We’re cash house buyers who know that each situation and each seller is different. We can assure you that no amount of damage or clutter is going to bother us. We even take care of moving any unwanted items for homeowners after the sale is complete. From minor repairs to major ones, we see them all—no vacuuming or hole-filling required.

Once we visit your house on a day that works for you, we take a quick look around the property, then take a moment to chat about how our process fits with your situation. If we make a fair cash offer for your house, we recommend you take some time to think it over, talk with your family or trusted advisors, and come back to us with an answer when you’re ready.
